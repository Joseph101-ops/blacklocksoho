About Us

Blacklock Soho is a modern steakhouse inspired by the old chophouses of London. We focus on high-quality meat, slow cooking, and rich flavours served in a warm, industrial-style underground dining space. Every dish is prepared with precision using premium cuts sourced from trusted farms. Whether you're here for a casual dinner, a celebration, or a weekend feast, our goal is simple — to deliver one of the best steak experiences in London. We believe in simplicity done perfectly: great meat, great service, and a great atmosphere.
